How does FREEgiftworld work?

Step 1 : Choose a free gift and enter your email address.

You will receive a confirmation email sent to the email address you specified. Please verify the spelling to insure accuracy. For your protection, we cannot ship your free gift unless you activate your account by clicking on the account activation link provided in the confirmation email and following the activation instructions.

After you click on the account activation link and enter a new password, your account will be established. Make sure that you save your password. You will need this password to check on the status of your free gift.

Step 2: Complete sponsor offers

Sponsors cover the entire cost of your free gift, plus all the shipping & handling fees. By "completing an offer", we mean that you take delivery of the product or service provided by the sponsor. For credit card offers, you must be approved for and activate that credit card. Activation means completing a balance transfer or using your new credit card for a purchase. A pop-up window will appear after you click on a sponsor's offer with details on how to complete a particular offer.

It takes up to 60 days after you complete an offer before our sponsor notifies us. Once we receive this notification, your account will be updated accordingly.

Please note: Earning some free gifts requires you to complete more than one sponsor offer. Each gift has a Level which indicates the required number of sponsor offers that you must complete:

Level 1 - Complete any 1 Sponsor Offer
Level 2 - Complete any 2 Sponsor Offers
Level 3 - Complete any 3 Sponsor Offers
Level 4 - Complete any 3 Sponsor Offers and 1 Premium Sponsor Offer

Registered members can access the Level of each gift through their account at www.freegiftworld.com . Please note that all completed sponsor offers expire 60 days after posting to your account. Review the Terms and Conditions for complete program details.

Step 3: Tell us where to send your free gift

Check your gift status by accessing your account through www.freegiftworld.com. When you have met all of the requirements to earn your free gift, your gift status will be marked "Approved" on the gift status screen, which is accessible to registered members. To receive your gift, you must click on the "Approved" hyperlink, confirm your shipping address, print a copy of the Gift Redemption Certificate, and mail the certificate, postmarked within 30 days, to the address below:
